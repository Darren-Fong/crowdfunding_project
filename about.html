Our Mission

Tired of picking your outfit before work every single day? We have a solution — by integrating AI technology into a mirror, we're aiming to revolutionalize their usage, making them become your personal assistant. Imagine your childhood self asking your parents for outfit recommendations. That's exactly what Smart Mirror does, except better. We are honing our technology to give you personal fashion recommendations based on weather and your emotion, using AI analysis and previous interactions.
